Custom Production · SMB

A custom-case manufacturer drowning in chat messages

The challenge

Every order lived across Telegram, WhatsApp, voice notes, and people's memory. Orders stalled on the shop floor with no owner, status was impossible to read, and the business scaled only as far as the founder could personally track it.

What we built

A single Bitrix24 order pipeline with a dedicated production smart-process — stages, owners, and deadlines per workshop step. Mobile status updates for floor staff, automated task creation, SLA escalations, and AI summaries of long client chats and voice notes.
